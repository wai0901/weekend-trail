Three easy coastal routes, perfect for a slow weekend walk with your partner. No crowds, just ocean breeze.

Dana Point Headlands

Conservation Area · Orange County

🌊

沿住海岸懸崖頂嘅輕鬆步道，視野極開揚，望到成個 Dana Point 海灣。幾條小路交織，適合慢漫影相。

~2 miles Easy Coastal Views

Bolsa Chica Reserve

Ecological Reserve · Huntington Beach

🌿

全程平坦木棧道，濕地生態豐富，好多雀鳥。風景寧靜，行完可以直接去海灘睇日落。

~1.5 miles Very Easy Bird Watching
Navigate

Crystal Cove

Bluff Top Trail · Laguna Beach

🏖️

沿懸崖邊行嘅海岸徑，海景一流。行完可以落去海灘或者去旁邊嘅 Shake Shack 食嘢。週末氣氛好好。

~1-2 miles Easy Ocean Cliff
